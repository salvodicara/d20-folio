The Hide action now plays itself (2024 rules): the card teaches the real rule — a DC 15
Dexterity (Stealth) check that makes you Invisible — and carries a d20 roll-entry that folds
your live Stealth bonus, applies the Invisible condition on a success, and remembers your
check total as the DC for a creature to find you (shown right on the Invisible chip until the
condition ends — including when a DM clears it from the shared encounter). The whole outcome
is one undoable step; a failed check is a quiet notice.
The expanded card also lists when hiding ends (attacking, a Verbal-component spell, loud
noise, or being found).
