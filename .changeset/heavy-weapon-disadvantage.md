Heavy weapons now warn you when your Strength or Dexterity is too low to wield them well. Under the
2024 rules a Heavy weapon gives Disadvantage on attack rolls if your Strength is below 13 (for a
melee weapon) or your Dexterity is below 13 (for a ranged one). Both weapon surfaces — the combat
action card and the inventory weapon card — now show a quiet advisory note the moment that applies,
computed from your effective scores, so a set-Strength item (Gauntlets of Ogre Power) correctly
clears it. The note disappears as soon as the relevant score reaches 13. No dice are rolled — the
app only reminds you the roll has Disadvantage.
