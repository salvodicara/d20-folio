Added Mounted Combat and Underwater Combat quick-references. Like the existing Cover reference, these
2024 rules (SRD 5.2.1) depend on the battlefield situation and have no per-character value to compute,
so the app now carries them as ready-to-show reference facts in both English and Italian: how mounting
or dismounting costs half your Speed, what a controlled versus an independent mount can do, the DC 10
Dexterity save to avoid falling off, and underwater's melee and ranged Disadvantage rules plus Fire
resistance. No dice are rolled and nothing about your character changes.
