Weapon Mastery now does its own arithmetic (2024 rules): the Topple chip prints your live save
DC ("Topple · DC 14") and the Graze chip your on-miss damage ("Graze · 3") on both the combat
and inventory weapon cards, and a Nick-mastered off-hand attack now correctly rides the Attack
action instead of costing your Bonus Action (it appears with the free actions once you've
attacked with a Light weapon). The Light property still grants only one off-hand attack per
turn, so taking either off-hand attack marks any other off-hand weapon used for the turn.
