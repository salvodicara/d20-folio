When you're Prone, the turn meter now offers a one-tap Stand up button that clears the condition and
spends half your Speed of movement (the 2024 rule). One tap, fully undoable — the clear and the
movement cost revert together — and the meter reminds you that crawling costs extra movement while
you stay down. No dice are rolled; the app just applies the deterministic cost you can undo.
