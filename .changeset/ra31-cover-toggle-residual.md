docs(audit): adjudicate RA-31 — the self-side Cover AC/DEX-save toggle is DECLINED, cover stays a triaged residual

The last open design question of the 2024 core-rules audit, resolved per golden rule 26 (the
impeccable skill as deciding authority). The +2/+5 self-side toggle is declined because cover has
no rules-defined lifetime the engine can enforce (a sticky chip teaches a stale AC; a turn-scoped
clear removes the bonus exactly before its enemy-turn relevance window), cover is the DM's
per-attack ruling so a sheet-baked bonus double-counts, and the app's settled precedent for the
nearest universal-defensive sibling (Dodge) is reference-only. The knowledge gap the FOR case
identified is already served by the W6 Rules-reference panel (`SituationalRules`), where the Cover
table renders on the Play tab. The ledger entry records the full verdict, the triaged-residuals
list absorbs it, and MECHANICS.md's battlefield-geometry residual carries the standing record.
