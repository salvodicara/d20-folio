The combat action list now carries the full set of 2024 generic actions. Influence, Magic, Study,
and Utilize join Dash, Dodge, Search and the rest as pinnable base-action cards, and the Search card
is corrected to the 2024 Wisdom skills (Insight, Medicine, Perception, Survival) — Investigation now
lives on the new Study card, where the 2024 rules put it. Each card states which check or effect the
action uses, in English and Italian, so the whole 2024 action economy is one tap away when you build
your turn. No dice are rolled — the app shows the rule, you make the roll.
