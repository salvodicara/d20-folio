The "d20 Folio" footer wordmark is gold again in the light theme. A prior change had
re-inked it in a deep bronze that read as unreadable dark text on the daylight plates;
per the owner's ruling that treatment was a regression ("that dark bronze looks horrible
on light, it's unreadable"), so the wordmark returns to its gold-leaf gilt — honey-on-honey
on the light footer is fine and reads better than the bronze. The dark theme was never
affected.
