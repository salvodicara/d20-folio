feat(sheet): show how far your character can jump (RA-26)

Your sheet now displays your Long Jump (your Strength score in feet) and High Jump (3 + your
Strength modifier). Both were already computed by the rules engine but never displayed; they now sit
as two rows in the movement and senses rail beside your other speeds, shown in feet (EN) or metres
(IT). The values read your effective Strength, so a set-Strength magic item raises them. No dice are
rolled.
