The attack-roll tooltip on every attack card now explains what a Critical Hit does. Tapping the
to-hit hint (on weapon, spell, and other attack rows) now adds the 2024 rule: on a Critical Hit you
roll all the attack's damage dice twice — including extra dice like Sneak Attack — and total them,
adding your modifier only once. English and Italian both included. No dice are rolled; the app states
the rule so you roll it right at the table.
