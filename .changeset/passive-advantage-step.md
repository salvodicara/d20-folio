Passive Perception, Insight, and Investigation now apply the 2024 ±5 advantage/disadvantage step.
Under the 2024 rules a passive score is +5 when you have Advantage on the underlying check and −5 with
Disadvantage. A character wielding a Sentinel Shield (or a Rod of Alertness / an Awareness item) now
sees the +5 reflected in passive Perception on the sheet, the party dashboard, and the PDF — and the
score's breakdown tip shows the Advantage line so the number still explains itself. A same-source
Disadvantage cancels the step (straight roll), and a manual override still wins. The step only folds an
advantage that names the exact passive's check; situational sight-only or broad ability-check
advantages are intentionally left out for now.
