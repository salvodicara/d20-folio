Golden rule 15 now mandates that owner-pushed screenshots be FUNCTIONAL FOR MONITORING: each shot
is CROPPED/zoomed to the region the change actually affects and delivered as a BEFORE/AFTER pair
wherever a prior state exists, so the diff reads at a glance instead of forcing the owner to hunt
through a whole-page shot — a full-page shot is right only when the change IS the whole page (a new
layout, theme, or full redesign) (owner, 2026-07-21). Rule 25 and the `docs/CONTRIBUTING.md`
rule-25-shots note point at rule 15 for the crop + before/after requirement so the docs stay
consistent.
