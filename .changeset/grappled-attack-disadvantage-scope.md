The turn-limiter summary no longer says a Grappled character has Disadvantage on all attacks. Under
the 2024 rules the Grappled condition gives Disadvantage on attack rolls only against targets other
than the grappler; the "what's limiting you this turn" line now states that scope, while conditions
with a genuine blanket penalty (Poisoned, Prone, Frightened, Restrained, Blinded) are unchanged. No
dice are rolled — the app only tells you the roll has Disadvantage and against whom.
