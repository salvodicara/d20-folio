Fix the mobile palette tap that "did nothing": tapping a search result in Ask the Folio now always opens the target (compendium entry, character, campaign, section). The palette's Back-sentinel retirement (`history.back()`) is an async traversal, and the old two-animation-frame deferral pushed the navigation while it was still in flight — the landing traversal then rewound the fresh route, silently undoing the navigation (mobile frame timing lost that race most of the time; desktop usually won it). The shared overlay-history seam now exposes `retireTopOverlayThen`, which runs the navigation on the traversal's popstate — its one deterministic completion signal. Every member of the race class routes through it: the palette's result navigation, its shortcuts-sheet and bug-reporter raises (whose fresh sentinel the landing traversal could otherwise consume — hardware Back would then exit the page instead of closing them), and the campaign create/join modals' hub navigation (clearing their dead same-key Back entry too). The seam serializes every history mutation behind the in-flight traversal (covering StrictMode's remount re-push), cancels a queued sentinel whose overlay unmounts before the traversal lands, and drops the queue on a throwing op so nothing replays under a later unrelated traversal.
