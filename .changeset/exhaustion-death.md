Exhaustion level 6 now shows the character as fallen, everywhere. Under the 2024 rules a creature
dies at Exhaustion 6; the app treated it as a display-only line. It now folds into the one shared
"fallen" verdict — the roster tile dims, hides HP, and shows the Fallen tag, and the cockpit's
Exhaustion strip reads the same "Dead" word the death-save track uses. It is fully reversible: lower
the Exhaustion track below 6 and the character is alive again, with no stored flag to undo.
