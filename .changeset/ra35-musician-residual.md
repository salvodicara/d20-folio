Closed the RA-35 audit item: the Musician feat's Encouraging Song gives Heroic Inspiration to your
allies (up to your Proficiency Bonus), not to you. Since the app models a single character there are
no ally targets to grant, and your own Heroic Inspiration is already a one-tap toggle on every sheet,
so nothing new needs computing. The feat's text is already correct for the 2024 rules in English and
Italian; this is a documentation-only reconciliation with no behavior change.
