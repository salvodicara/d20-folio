Made the Combat tab's two reference blocks on-demand. The combat playbook and the SRD rules
reference (Cover, Mounted, Underwater, Travel Pace) now sit collapsed to just their header at the
foot of the tab; click a header and its full body blooms open in place. Each section remembers
whether you left it open or closed, per device, so it stays the way you set it across tab switches
and reloads. Nothing about your character changes and no dice are rolled.
