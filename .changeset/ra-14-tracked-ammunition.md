Ranged weapons now track their ammunition. A bow, crossbow, sling, or blowgun with the Ammunition
property shows its live remaining count as a stat row on the attack card ("Arrows · 18"), reading
straight from your inventory, and firing debits one piece per attack (undo puts it straight back).
Tracking is entirely opt-in: if you don't carry a matching ammo item, nothing is counted, debited,
or shown — so untracked play is unchanged. When the quiver hits zero (or a Loading weapon would fire
a second shot in one action) the attack button dims with a quiet advisory but STILL taps, since you
might be carrying ammo the app doesn't track. Adds the two missing SRD ammo items — Sling Bullets and
Blowgun Needles — so every ranged weapon has its ammunition (EN + IT).
