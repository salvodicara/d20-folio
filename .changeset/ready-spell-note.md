The Ready action card now teaches the readied-spell rule instead of showing a bare "set trigger"
line. Expanding the card explains that to ready a spell you cast it now from its own card — the slot
is spent and you hold it with Concentration until you release it with your Reaction. The card just
bridges you to that route; the real cast (and its Concentration) is the spell's own card on the same
board. English and Italian both included.
