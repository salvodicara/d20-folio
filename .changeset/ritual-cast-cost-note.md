Ritual spells now show a "+10 min · no slot" cost note beside the Ritual cast button, so the
10-minute ritual trade-off is clear before you cast (RA-24). The note appears exactly on the spells
that can be ritual-cast — a prepared ritual, or any ritual on a Wizard's sheet — and never on
cantrips or non-ritual spells. No dice are rolled.
